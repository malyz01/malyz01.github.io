Sprint 3 - Cultural
Research and discuss EQ and IQ
What is Emotional Intelligence?
It mostly involves perceiving, understanding, and regulating emotions. It is also about the ability to reason and solve problems based on the emotions we experience.
How is it different to IQ?
EQ deals more on emotions while IQ deals with logical reasoning, word comprehenssion, math skills, and information skills.
Why is it important?
It can be advantageous as individuals with high EQ usually make great leaders and team players because of their ability to understand, empathize, and connect with the people around them
