Thinking like a programmer
Describe the process for solving problems
build a habit - setting a todo list everyday to finish and if possible to do more, understand - try to translate the problem at hand in the most simple way possible, plan - take considerations of all the possible problem angles you will face, divide - try to breakdown difficult problems into a much more solvable problems, make an attempt at solving it - try solving it, stuck - locate the source of the problem then try a different solution. Do some research, a person in the internet might already solve your problem. Refactor - make your code readable inorder to find problems a lot quicker, reflect - review all the things you have done and summarize the things the needs improving, practice - keep on practicing until it will become second nature to you.
Will you follow that process? How?
Yes. By following what I described above.
What did you learn about flipped classroom?
It is where lectures or learning materials are sent out for students to access anytime during their free time. This would create an opportunity for teachers to let their students ask them questions regarding those lectures. By doing so, it helps both the student and teacher in managing their time and effort to learn.
